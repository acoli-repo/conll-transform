CoNLL-03 sample data

description: https://www.clips.uantwerpen.be/conll2003/ner/
source: https://www.clips.uantwerpen.be/conll2003/ner/

format:
- SPACE-separated
- columns: WORD POS CHUNK NER
- CHUNK: IOB (like CoNLL-00)
- NER: IOB (like CoNLL-00)

Note that the actual training data deviates from the specification and several languages lack the POS column
